# Blog Cover Image Credits

Permanent licence record for the blog cover photos in this directory. Every
photo below is sourced from Unsplash and is used under the **Unsplash License**
(<https://unsplash.com/license>), which permits commercial use without
attribution. Attribution is recorded here anyway so the provenance of every
image on a commercial site is auditable.

Do not add an image to this directory without adding a row here first. If a
licence cannot be stated, the post keeps its branded `.svg` fallback.

## Licence

All 14 photos: **Unsplash License** — "Free to use under the Unsplash License",
as stated on each photo page listed below (verified 17 July 2026).

The Unsplash License grants free commercial use with no attribution required.
It does **not** grant trademark or personality rights, so photos dominated by
third-party logos or by an identifiable named individual were rejected during
selection even where the licence itself was fine.

**Unsplash+ is a different, paid licence and is NOT used here.** Every photo
below was confirmed to resolve through Unsplash's public download endpoint,
which serves free-licence photos and refuses (HTTP 403) Unsplash+ content.
